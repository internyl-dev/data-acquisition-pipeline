Summer Opportunities for Future Innovators

Spend your summer pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence with our cutting-edge labs. Students will work alongside PhD mentors and present research at a virtual symposium.

We encourage applications from students rising to 11th or 12th grade who have demonstrated interest in STEM.

  • Program runs: June 24 - August 16
  • Online and in-person (New York City)
  • Stipends available for all qualifying students

Applications due by midnight on May 20, 2025. No late submissions will be considered.
